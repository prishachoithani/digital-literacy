# Social Media Do's and Don'ts

## Do's

- Think before posting and consider the long-term impact on your reputation
- Maintain a professional and respectful online profile
- Verify facts before sharing any information
- Respect others' privacy and seek consent before posting
- Use privacy settings to control visibility of your content
- Engage in positive and constructive discussions
- Balance social media usage with academic responsibilities

---

## Don'ts

- Do not overshare personal or sensitive information
- Avoid posting in anger or emotional distress
- Do not spread misinformation or rumors
- Avoid cyberbullying, trolling, or harmful comments
- Do not ignore your digital footprint and its consequences
- Avoid comparing yourself excessively with others online
- Do not click on suspicious links or fall for online scams
